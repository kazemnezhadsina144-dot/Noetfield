FAQ.

Clear answers on pricing, timeline, revisions, privacy, integrations, and ownership.

Is this an AI receptionist service or a missed-call recovery system?+
The core offer is missed-call recovery: call capture, intake, follow-up automation, and booking handoff. AI receptionist is optional and only used if it improves operations without degrading customer experience.
What do I get in the “5-Day QuickScan”?+
A short operational review plus a deployed flow: routing rules, intake capture, message templates, test plan, and a go-live handoff. It is designed to be implemented quickly and measured with basic KPIs.
Do you guarantee more revenue or a specific number of booked jobs?+
No. The work aims to improve capture rate, response time, and operational follow-through within scope. Market demand, pricing, and dispatch capacity are outside direct control, so no revenue guarantees are offered.
How fast can we go live?+
Standard delivery is “live in 5 days” once access is available (phone routing/forwarding, messaging tool, intake form destination). If access is delayed, the go-live date shifts accordingly.
What phone system do I need?+
Most setups can work with your current provider via forwarding and routing rules. If needed, Twilio can be used for more control. The simplest viable option is chosen during intake.
How do integrations work (Calendly, Airtable, Zapier/Make, CRM)?+
Starter uses minimal integration. Standard adds booking handoff and basic reporting. Extended can integrate with Airtable and automation tools (Zapier/Make) within the agreed scope. Deep CRM migrations are excluded unless separately scoped.
Is call recording required? What about transcripts and retention?+
Recording is optional. Transcripts are off by default. If enabled, retention is limited (default 14 days) and can be adjusted. Privacy defaults are designed to reduce risk while still enabling quality control.
Who owns the accounts and the setup?+
You do. The system is configured in client-owned accounts wherever possible. Documentation is included so the office can operate the flow without dependency.
What support is included after go-live?+
Two revision rounds within 7 days of go-live for scripts, intake fields, and routing logic within scope. Ongoing support/optimization can be added as a separate monthly arrangement if needed.
What if I want to cancel or pause?+
If cancelled before work begins, only payment processing fees may apply. If work is in progress, fees reflect completed milestones. Exact terms are confirmed in the invoice/booking terms.
Is this legal advice or compliance advice?+
No. This is an operational system for call capture and follow-up. Any legal/compliance questions should be reviewed with qualified counsel for your jurisdiction and business context.
