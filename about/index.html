Operating principles

The stance that governs what goes into the Brief and the Ledger.

  1. Materiality first: govern what matters (decisions), not everything (experiments).
  2. Clarity over exhaustiveness: a short, accurate ledger beats a large, ignored catalogue.
  3. Evidence-first: if it cannot be supported, it does not go into the ledger.
  4. Reusable outputs: artefacts must travel into board packs, audit files, and oversight conversations.
