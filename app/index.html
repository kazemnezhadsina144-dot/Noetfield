Security & privacy posture (overview)

The App is a controlled deal room. Sensitive materials move only after scope and a secure channel are agreed. Public intake is routing-only.

What is allowed in public intake routing-only
  • High-level need and preferred package
  • Business contact information
  • Non-sensitive context required to route the request
What is not allowed in public intake do not submit
  • Credentials, tokens, secrets, private keys
  • Regulated personal data or sensitive content
  • Production exports unless explicitly requested via a secure channel

What “board-ready” means acceptance-led
  • Executive summary stands alone (decision options + recommendation)
