When to reach out

The contact form is intended for stewards and teams with responsibility for AI decisions in real-economy sectors – boards, executives, regulators, and internal governance functions.

  • You are preparing for a board or committee discussion about AI.
  • A supervisor has started to ask detailed questions about AI governance.
  • AI already influences credit, pricing, onboarding, trading, or critical operations.
  • You need a concise artefact to connect technical systems with board-level accountability.

If your question is more general or exploratory, it is still welcome, but priority is given to live governance, risk, and supervisory contexts.
