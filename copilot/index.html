2) Process

60-second runbook (buyer-visible)

Designed to reduce internal friction between IT, Security, Legal, and Procurement.

  • Non-confidential intake: scope intent + stakeholder map + access posture (no secrets).
  • Scope lock: explicit boundary (who/where/what) + assumptions + decision constraints.
  • Evidence requests: minimal, prioritized collection (procurement-safe checklist).
  • Checkpoint: align owners + “must-not-expose” surfaces + connector/extension posture.
  • Decision pack: findings + risk hotspots + 30/60/90 plan + proceed/pause/remediate options.
Measured on outcomes: confirmed risk-reduction actions + decision readiness, not document volume.
3) Deliverables

Board-ready, procurement-forwardable

Outputs are built to be filed, forwarded, and referenced — not slide-heavy marketing.

Readiness (2/4/6 weeks)
Governance baseline + ownership + evidence discipline, packaged for executive and procurement use.
  • Governance baseline: policy + controls + owners (minimum viable governance).
  • Exposure surfaces map: priority hotspots and boundary conditions.
  • Control posture summary: what is on/off, allowed/blocked, reviewed/monitored.
  • Evidence pack: what exists vs missing, mapped to procurement/audit confidence.
  • Board memo: decision points + risk statement + recommended actions.
  • 30/60/90 plan: sequencing + owners + “go/no-go” gates.
QuickScan (5–7 days)
Rapid signal: scope lock + oversharing risk themes + decision options.
  • Scope lock: Copilot rollout boundary with explicit assumptions.
  • Oversharing heatmap: high-risk data surfaces and priority hotspots.
  • Sensitive boundaries: what must not be exposed + prompt/behavior guardrails.
  • Connector/extension notes: allow/block/review-first posture.
  • Evidence readiness: checklist of what procurement can file today vs gaps.
  • Decision options: proceed / pause / remediate, with a short action plan.
Upgrade logic: QuickScan reduces uncertainty. Readiness produces a decision-grade baseline and artifacts suitable for enterprise approval workflows.
