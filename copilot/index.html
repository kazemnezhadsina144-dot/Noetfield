FAQ (common objections)

Short answers to reduce back-and-forth and unblock decision-making.

Is any confidential information required in the intake?
No. Public intake is non-confidential by design. Read-only review is preferred. Content sampling is not performed unless explicitly authorized in writing.
What exactly do we receive at the end?
A named evidence pack (D0–D5) with an executive summary, exposure paths, boundary rules, evidence readiness steps, and owners — plus a 30/60/90 plan.
Can this be completed without full admin access?
Often yes, but scope becomes narrower. Read-only administrative access is the fastest path because it enables export of configuration posture and reporting signals.
Is this an implementation project?
No. QuickScan is assessment + evidence readiness. Implementation/remediation can be scoped as a separate sprint after the evidence pack is delivered.
What if we are not ready to go live?
QuickScan still produces a defensible posture and a ranked plan. The output is designed to support a safe “pause + fix” decision, not force go-live.
