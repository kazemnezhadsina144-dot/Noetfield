Scope boundaries

Designed to avoid procurement friction and change-order ambiguity.

In scope Out of scope (by default)
Governance baseline, ownership, evidence expectations, decision artifacts, adoption sequencing. Implementation/remediation engineering, tenant reconfiguration work, long training programs, custom development.
Risk themes and guardrails suitable for leadership approval and procurement filing. Full security penetration testing, forensics, or incident response services.
Implementation note: Remediation/execution can be scoped after delivery if required.
Inputs required

Minimal meetings, clear owners, controlled access approach.

  • Accountable owner: one person to confirm scope and approve access approach.
  • Read-only access preferred: posture review and evidence export steps.
  • Boundary agreement: what content is in-scope vs out-of-scope for Copilot reach.
  • Stakeholder touchpoints: typically 2–4 short sessions (tier-dependent).
Commercials / Procurement

Structured to reduce purchasing friction.

  • Pricing validity: 60 days from quote date (unless otherwise stated).
  • Purchase paths: Invoice/PO (preferred for enterprise) or card payment (optional).
