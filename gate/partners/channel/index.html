What channel partners do

Bring the right work, protect the signal, keep it decisive.

Identify fit
Organizations where AI influences material decisions (risk, compliance, finance, HR, operations).
Qualify fast
Confirm scope, urgency, buyer role, and whether evidence exists (or can exist).
Route delivery
Connect the client to the right lane: Delivery / Integration, with clean handoffs.
Protect evidence
Keep the work board-safe: clear claims, traceable proofs, and minimal “hype artifacts.”

Best-fit profiles

Channel is about routing and qualification — not technical implementation.

  • B2B agencies that already sell governance, security, IT, or data work
  • Fractional leaders (COO/CISO/GC/Compliance) with real operator networks
  • Advisors who can access board-level conversations and decision-makers
  • Operators with deep vertical access (finance, construction, healthcare, logistics, education)
Useful context: Trust Brief · Trust Ledger · For Whom

How deals flow

A simple routing system that keeps quality high.

1) Intake

You submit a partner intake with your profile + how you source opportunities (non-confidential).

2) Fit rubric

We align on what “qualified” means: buyer role, urgency, scope, and evidence availability.

3) Routing

Each opportunity routes to the right lane: Delivery or Integration.

4) Board-ready outputs

Trust Brief + Trust Ledger entries are produced with traceable claims and an evidence trail.
