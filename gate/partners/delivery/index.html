Best-fit profiles

Delivery is a discipline: writing + governance logic + evidence hygiene.

  • Governance / compliance consultants who can produce board-ready memos
  • Security, privacy, and risk teams that can translate controls into auditable artifacts
  • Policy/ops operators who can map process reality into defensible documentation
  • Technical writers / analysts with evidence capture discipline
Reference artefacts: Trust Brief · Trust Ledger · Playbook

Delivery standards

What “board-ready” means in practice.

Evidence discipline

Claims must map to proofs (logs, configs, exports, policies, screenshots, verification steps).

Scope clarity

Clear inclusions/exclusions, deliverable list, acceptance criteria, and assumptions.

Decision framing

Every artifact ends with options, tradeoffs, recommended path, and what happens next.

Audit survival

The package remains understandable 12 months later by a new reviewer (board/regulator).
