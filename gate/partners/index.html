Deal protection terms (compact)

Terms designed to reduce channel conflict and keep co-selling defensible. Acceptance is confirmed by email after basic validation.

Acceptance
Protection begins when Noetfield sends a Deal Accepted email after validating minimum fields.
Protection window
90 days from acceptance, provided active progression signals remain current.
Minimum fields
Org name, champion, stage, next meeting date, lane, and a short non-confidential note.
Review SLA
Response within 2 business days (Accept / Clarify / Decline).
Conflicts
First complete registration timestamp plus evidence of progression (meeting scheduled/held, buyer-confirmed path).
Scope boundary
Protection applies to the named organization and registered opportunity; public tenders may override.
Public boundary: do not submit credentials, tenant identifiers, customer content, or regulated personal data. Sensitive context begins only after NDA + executed SOW (via Procurement lane).

RID vs Deal ID (do not mix)

RID tracks routing across Gate → Procurement → Status. Deal ID tracks partner-originated opportunities.

  • Use RID in: subjects, filenames, status checks, procurement packets.
  • Use Deal ID in: deal registration, co-sell tracking, partner protection records.
  • Buyer procurement: route procurement teams to Invoice / PO lane.

Standards (non-negotiables)

Rules that keep delivery quality defensible.

  • Public = non-confidential: no secrets/credentials/customer data/regulated personal data in forms.
  • Named artefacts: memo/control taxonomy/evidence index formats remain consistent.
  • Evidence IDs: material claims require traceable references (export, screenshot, policy, log).
  • Red-lines: escalate blockers fast (privacy/security/procurement) and document disposition.
  • RID discipline: use RID in subjects, filenames, and status checks.

Partner FAQ (compact)

What counts as acceptance for protection?
Protection starts when a Deal Accepted email is issued after minimum fields validate (org, champion, stage, next meeting date, lane). Incomplete registrations return Clarify.
What happens if two partners register the same organization?
Resolution uses first complete registration timestamp plus evidence of progression (meeting scheduled/held, buyer-confirmed path).
Where should procurement teams go (Invoice/PO)?
Route to Procurement (Invoice/PO) for vendor onboarding, purchasing lanes, and procurement pack.
How does the Programs lane work?
Two models: program contract (portfolio package) or company-by-company routing. See Programs lane and Portfolio offer.
Decision rule: repeatable delivery, clean scope boundaries, and evidence capture habits that scale.
