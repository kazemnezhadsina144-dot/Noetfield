What this partner track is (and is not)

Use this track when the goal is joint delivery, channel fit, or governed co-selling.

  • Purpose: scale Trust Brief + Trust Ledger delivery through aligned operators.
  • Outputs: board-ready artefacts; plain-language memos; evidence pointers; routing.
  • Fit: security, privacy, risk, audit, M365/Copilot governance, vendor risk, policy writing.
  • Not fit: generic “AI consulting,” chatbot builds, marketing automation, or hype-first bundles.
Delivery bar: write as if a board, regulator, or audit committee will read it. Start non-confidential; move to safer channels after alignment.

Minimum expectations

  • Operate inside client governance constraints (audit/risk/legal/security).
  • Evidence capture discipline (“what exists vs what is claimed”).
  • Clean comms: crisp, non-technical, decision-grade writing.
  • Respect boundaries: no public solicitation via this track.

How joint work typically runs

Simple, controlled, artefact-driven.

  • Step 1: intake (capabilities, coverage, proof).
  • Step 2: standards alignment (templates, language, evidence fields).
  • Step 3: first joint pilot (Trust Brief delivery or Ledger build).
  • Step 4: repeat and scale.
Commercial model: referral, rev-share, or subcontracting depending on lane — only after fit is validated.
