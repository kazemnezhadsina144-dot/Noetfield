Fit criteria

Repeatable implementation with proof discipline.

Identity
SSO/IdP (Entra ID, Okta, etc.) with admin boundaries, role separation, and clear ownership.
Audit logs
Export + retention + traceability for material actions and configuration changes.
DLP & labels
Classification, protection, and enforcement patterns (Purview/MIP or equivalent).
Controls
Conditional access, MFA, device compliance, least privilege, change control, drift prevention.

Typical scopes

  • SSO rollout + conditional access baseline (documented exceptions)
  • Audit log pipeline + retention policy + evidence extraction steps
  • DLP + classification labels + enforcement patterns
  • Admin role separation + change control and rollback discipline
  • Acceptance report: what changed, proof types, and re-verification steps
