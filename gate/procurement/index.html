Procurement FAQ

Short answers aligned to common enterprise procurement questions.

Is card payment required?
No. Card is optional via /gate/sales/. Procurement can proceed via invoice, PO, or vendor onboarding.
Does Noetfield sign NDAs and DPAs?
NDA/MSA/SOW/DPA are supported as required by buyer policy. Send documents to procurement@noetfield.com with the Request ID.
What should be sent before kickoff?
Only high-level context and internal process requirements. Do not send secrets/credentials via public channels. Secure options are confirmed after kickoff when required.
How is delivery structured?
Asynchronous-first delivery with board/procurement-ready artifacts. A short call is available if your policy requires live scope confirmation.
