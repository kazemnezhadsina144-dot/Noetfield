Do you accept invoice / PO? ▾

Yes. Use Procurement to receive a vendor snapshot and an invoicing path aligned to your process. Request ID keeps the thread consistent across internal approvals.

Is pricing fixed? ▾

Indicative ranges are shown for procurement and early alignment. Final scope is confirmed after intake based on materiality, number of business units, tool footprint, and delivery depth.

How is confidentiality handled? ▾

Public intake is non-confidential. Sensitive details are not submitted via public forms. Any confidential exchange is handled in a controlled engagement path after kickoff.

What is the fastest entry? ▾

Copilot QuickScan is the fastest entry product when Copilot adoption is in scope. For broader AI governance baseline, Trust Brief is the diagnostic lane.
