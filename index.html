Who it is for

Boards, executives, and stewards under AI pressure

Noetfield is designed for organisations where AI is beginning to matter – in risk, operations, or customer journeys – and where questions from boards, funders, or regulators are becoming more frequent.

Governance & risk

Board chairs, risk and audit committees, and trustees who need a short, credible view of AI risk and oversight that they can read in minutes and revisit each cycle.

Oversight

Supervisory teams and funders who want a concise, consistent way to see how AI is controlled, without requesting and reconciling dozens of underlying artefacts every time.

Why now

From “Do you use AI?” to “How is it governed?”

Across sectors, the questions are shifting. Boards, funders, and regulators are no longer satisfied with a simple inventory or a model card. They want to understand how AI decisions relate to fiduciary duties, customer impact, and sectoral expectations.

Most organisations already have fragments: logs, prompts, dashboards, validation reports, policies, and minutes. Noetfield’s work is to connect those fragments into one narrative that can be trusted and reused.

The most direct entry point is a single Trust Brief pilot. From there, the Trust Ledger can grow alongside your AI footprint and regulatory context – at a pace that matches your actual risk, not the hype cycle.
