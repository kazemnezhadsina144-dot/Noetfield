Partner Notes Program

Structured, high-risk early exposure for aligned partners – not retail investors.

The Noetfield Partner Notes Program is a small, invitation-only track for sophisticated backers who want early exposure to Noetfield’s AI governance and Trust Ledger platform. Notes are unsecured, convertible, and tied directly to the first third-party priced equity round – with clear downside structure and disciplined upside.

Structure

How the Partner Notes work

Convertible, governance-first

Partner Notes are unsecured, high-risk convertible instruments. They are designed for a small group of aligned partners who understand that early-stage governance infrastructure is binary: either a durable layer is built, or capital is lost.

Each Note carries simple interest, a fixed term, and converts automatically into equity at a discount in the first third-party priced round. A valuation cap anchors the conversion, and certain tiers include a small warrant-like equity kicker in recognition of early conviction and strategic support.

  • Unsecured, high-risk, illiquid Partner Note – not a deposit and not a savings product.
  • Simple annual interest, accrued – no monthly payments.
  • Automatic conversion into the first third-party priced equity round at a 30% discount.
  • Valuation cap to protect downside relative to later valuations.
  • For larger, strategic tickets, an additional equity kicker via a small warrant.
In one sentence: the Partner Notes give sophisticated backers structured downside and disciplined upside tied to the first real equity price a professional investor is willing to pay for Noetfield.
Risk, suitability, and scope

Partner Notes are only suitable for investors who can afford to lose 100% of their capital and who are familiar with early-stage technology and infrastructure risk.

They are not designed for retail investors, for capital that needs near-term liquidity, or for anyone seeking guaranteed or low-volatility returns.

Illustrative mechanics

How the discount and cap work in practice

The Partner Notes are not priced as a fixed equity percentage. Instead, they convert into the same class of shares as the first third-party priced round, on more favourable economics than new cash investors.

  • If, for example, a professional investor leads a round at a 10M CAD pre-money valuation, new cash investors pay that price per share.
  • A Partner Note converts at a 30% discount to that price and, if applicable, at or below the agreed valuation cap.
  • The exact number of shares depends on the total amount raised, the final valuation, and the fully diluted capital structure at that time.

This keeps the economics disciplined and aligned with independent price discovery, rather than fixing percentages in advance or setting a standalone valuation for the notes.

Key principles

Across all tiers, the intent is consistent:

  • Protect the future cap table from being over-engineered at the note stage.
  • Respect the price-setting role of the first institutional or professional investors.
  • Reward early conviction with a clear discount, a cap, and, for larger tickets, a modest warrant kicker.
  • Keep documentation clean and compatible with future institutional capital.

FAQ

FAQ – Partner Notes in practice

Answer-ready
Is this a public solicitation or crowdfunding?
No. The information here is illustrative and high-level. Any actual Partner Note would only be offered to investors who qualify for relevant exemptions under applicable securities laws and would be documented in formal agreements reviewed by counsel. No general solicitation or crowdfunding is intended.
What kind of investor is a good fit?
Investors who are comfortable with early-stage technology and infrastructure risk, who understand that capital is illiquid and high-risk, and who are interested in the specific niche of AI governance and board-level trust infrastructure. For example, certain family offices, sophisticated individuals, or institutions with an innovation or stewardship mandate.
How many partners will you work with in this phase?
The Partner Notes Program is intentionally small. Noetfield prioritises quality of alignment over the number of participants and expects only a handful of partners at each tier, if any.
Why notes instead of direct equity now?
Partner Notes allow Noetfield to build the early governance layer, run pilots, and reach a more natural price discovery point with professional investors. They avoid fixing the full valuation too early or over-complicating the cap table at the pre-pilot stage, while still aligning early supporters with clear upside.
Do Partner Notes guarantee any specific return?
No. There are no guaranteed returns or liquidity commitments. Outcomes range from 0 (capital loss) to successful conversion into equity at a discount with additional upside if Noetfield grows and executes. Any scenario should be evaluated with independent legal, tax, and investment advice.
Can terms be customised for a specific institution?
Within the same core spine (simple interest, discount, cap, clean governance), certain parameters can be tuned for a strategic institution or family office, subject to legal advice and the need to keep the overall structure compatible with future institutional rounds.
