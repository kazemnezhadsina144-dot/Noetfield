FAQ

Short answers for internal alignment and procurement routing.

What is this page for?
A governance reference hub (0–90) showing the artefacts, evidence discipline, and cadence used across Noetfield deliveries.
What is the fastest paid start?
Copilot QuickScan. Broader programs (Trust Brief / Trust Ledger) typically start via Gate/procurement to confirm scope and cadence.
Do you support invoice / PO?
Yes. Use the procurement path or email procurement@noetfield.com. Public intake is non-confidential; secure sharing options are confirmed after kickoff.
What do you need from us to start?
Non-confidential intake details (use-cases, owners, constraints) plus a routing contact. Secure sharing options are confirmed after kickoff.
Is the Playbook available as a digital kit?
Yes—optional DIY kits are available for internal rollout. Enterprise delivery remains Copilot-first and procurement-scoped.
