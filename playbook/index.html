FAQ

Short answers for alignment and routing.

Are kits the same as services?
No. Kits are downloadable templates for internal execution. Services are scoped engagements that produce organization-specific outputs and procurement-ready routing.
How are kits delivered?
Kits are delivered via Gumroad after purchase (download + receipt email).
Is the hub confidential?
No. Public pages are designed for non-sensitive alignment. Secure sharing options are confirmed after kickoff where applicable.
Do you support invoice / PO?
Yes for services. Email procurement@noetfield.com for invoice/PO routing. Gumroad supports direct card purchase for kits.
