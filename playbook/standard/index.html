What’s included

Deliverables (Standard License)

Delivered as a structured document (PDF + editable formats) plus a small set of starter templates.

  • 0–90 day implementation pattern: what to do first, then next, then institutionalize.
  • AI scope & mandate: what “AI usage” means inside the team, and who owns decisions.
  • Use-case intake (lite): approve AI use-cases before adoption; escalation triggers included.
  • Data & privacy guardrails: what data can/cannot be shared with tools; safe defaults.
  • Vendor/tool due diligence (lite): a repeatable checklist to validate tools and evidence.
  • Incident & escalation pattern: what happens when outputs are wrong, harmful, or sensitive.
  • Board/client-ready brief template: 1–2 pages explaining governance posture in plain language.
  • Starter registers: a small risk register + AI tool register to keep governance visible.

Implementation

How teams implement this (typical)

  • Week 1: define scope, owners, and the top 3 AI use-cases in production.
  • Weeks 2–3: apply guardrails, create tool register + basic risk register, set escalation triggers.
  • Weeks 4–6: formalize “use-case intake” and publish the client-ready governance brief.
  • Weeks 7–12: extend to additional use-cases, refine evidence, prep for board/procurement scrutiny.
