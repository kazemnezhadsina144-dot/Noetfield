What’s included

Deliverables (Standard)

Delivered as a structured document (PDF + editable formats) plus templates you adapt to your team.

  • 0–90 day implementation pattern (practical sequencing + checkpoints).
  • AI use-case intake form (lite) (purpose, tool, data, risks, approval).
  • Data & privacy guardrails (what can / cannot be shared with AI tools).
  • Tool register v1 (approved tools + owners + allowed use).
  • Risk register v1 (small-team risks + mitigations + ownership).
  • Simple AI policy v1.0 (acceptable use + human-in-loop + incident triggers).
  • 1–2 page governance brief template (client/procurement-facing).
