Policy principles

How we keep policies readable and enforceable.

  • Clarity over legal theatre: plain language where possible.
  • Evidence-first: tracking keys (Request ID) and audit-friendly workflows.
  • Minimal collection: collect only what is needed to route and respond.
  • Security posture: no confidential uploads in public forms.
  • Versionable: pages can be updated with a visible “Last updated” date.
