What we need from you

This stays light-weight and non-disruptive, but it must be truthful.

  1. Point of contact for AI usage + IT/security context.
  2. Basic inventory of AI tools/vendors and key workflows.
  3. Access to sanitized artefacts (policies, screenshots, exports) when available.
  4. Agreement on what “material impact” means in your context.
