What Trust Ledger is

A maintained governance register for AI systems and AI-enabled workflows: what exists, who owns it, what controls apply, what evidence exists, what changed, what exceptions are active, and what the current decision posture is.

Purpose: stay export-ready
Mechanism: cadence + evidence discipline
Output: board/procurement packs

  • Change entries (scope, controls, providers)

FAQ

Short answers for internal alignment and procurement routing.

What is Trust Ledger?
A maintained register of AI systems, safeguards, sign-offs, exceptions, and evidence—kept ready for recurring scrutiny and board exports.
Do we need Trust Brief first?
Not always. Trust Brief is a structured diagnostic; Trust Ledger is the maintained cadence layer. Copilot-first baseline is recommended to set posture quickly.
Do you support invoice / PO?
Yes. Use the procurement path or email procurement@noetfield.com. Public intake is non-confidential; secure sharing options are confirmed after kickoff.
What should be shared in public forms?
Non-sensitive, non-confidential details only. Evidence transfer and secure sharing protocol are confirmed after kickoff.
