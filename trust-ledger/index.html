FAQ

Short answers for purchasing and internal routing.

What is an “active entry”?

One tracked AI system or AI-enabled workflow that has defined owners, controls, evidence hooks, and a change trail.

What does “setup” include?

Ledger schema baseline, initial inventory alignment, evidence reference conventions, and cadence confirmation.

Can procurement / invoice be used instead of card?

Yes. Procurement supports invoice/PO and internal approval routing. Scope and cadence can be confirmed after intake.

Is sensitive data required in public forms?

No. Public intake is for non-confidential details only. Secure sharing protocol is confirmed after kickoff.
