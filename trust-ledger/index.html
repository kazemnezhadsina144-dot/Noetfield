FAQ

Short answers for purchasing and internal routing.

What is an “active entry”?

One tracked AI system or AI-enabled workflow with defined owners, controls, evidence hooks, and a maintained change trail.

Card subscription vs invoice/PO: what is different?

Card subscription supports direct purchase via Stripe. Procurement supports invoice/PO and vendor onboarding. Final scope and kickoff are confirmed after routing.

Is sensitive data required in public forms?

No. Public intake is for non-confidential details only. Secure sharing protocol is confirmed after kickoff.

How does Trust Ledger relate to Copilot QuickScan and Trust Brief?

QuickScan establishes a rapid baseline for Copilot adoption exposure themes. Trust Brief establishes a deeper assessment baseline. Trust Ledger maintains posture over time with change control, exceptions, evidence discipline, and recurring exports.
