What these samples show

A board-safe way to record one material AI decision: scope, accountability, safeguards, evidence, and how incidents and changes are carried forward into board actions.

  • Human-readable, decision-first formatting (no deep technical detail required).
  • Clear ownership: business owner, risk owner, governance function.
  • Change and incident visibility across a reporting period.
  • Board actions: approvals, conditions, and follow-ups.

Typical report outline

  • Executive Summary (headlines + board decisions requested)
  • Material AI inventory snapshot
  • Significant changes (new / changed / decommissioned)
  • Incidents, exceptions, near-misses
  • Controls & testing (what exists, how often tested, results)
  • Provider & infrastructure view (where third parties matter)
  • Board actions & approvals (items, deadlines, conditions)
  • Annex (system profiles, mappings, glossary)
